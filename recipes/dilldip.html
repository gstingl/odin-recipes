Dill Pickle Dip

This is a super easy family favorite recipe if you love pickles. Served best with saltines or tortilla chips!


Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package of cream cheese
  • 1 cup chopped dill pickles
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped sweet onion
  • 2 tablespoons pickle juice, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Mix cream cheese in a bowl until smooth
  2. Stir in all ingredients
  3. Refrigerate at least one hour prior to serving
  4. Enjoy!
