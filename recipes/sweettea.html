Don's Sweet Tea!

How to brew Don's famous sweet tea!

This is a family favorite that is so simple, anyone can do it!


Cook's Note (before you start)

If and when you are using individual tea bags, your best option is to remove all the paper endings, and tie the strings together.


Ingredients

To start, please have these ingredients listed below:

  • 8 Cups Water
  • 4 Family-sized Tea Bags
  • 2 Cups White Sugar

Directions:

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot. Dissolve sugar in boiling water; Dissolve sugar in boiling water; remove from heat.
  2. Steep tea bags in water to desired strength of tea, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Refrigerate tea until cold, at least 1 hour.
  4. Enjoy!
